About Us

From 3D printing to textile crafting, Fab Lab provides the community with hands-on access to tools and environments that foster interdisciplinary collaboration and discovery. We invite educators, tinkerers, and inventors to unleash their natural creativity. Attend an event, reserve equipment, and start turning your idea into reality today!

Partners

Our partners elevate our community. We've mapped out touch points throughout the year that converge ideas and resources into powerful solutions for today's most pressing concerns. We invite a select few organizations to be at the center of these incredible moments, and take as much care in curating our partners as we do with our speakers and audience.
